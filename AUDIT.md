
## DentalScan – Technical & UX Audit

### Overview
I explored https://www.dentalscan.us/ as a real user and completed the full 5-angle Discovery Scan (Front, Left, Right, Upper, Lower). Overall, the UI is intuitive and the guided scanning flow is easy to follow, especially for first-time users. The step-by-step instructions and visual cues help reduce confusion during the process.

### What Works Well
- The guided scan flow is clear and structured.
- Auto-capture is a strong feature that reduces user effort.
- Visual instructions for different angles are helpful and easy to understand.
- The interface feels clean and responsive.

### Areas for Improvement

1. **Auto-Capture Sensitivity**
   - The auto-capture feature sometimes triggers even when the face is not properly centered or aligned.
   - This can result in inaccurate scans and may require retakes.

2. **Alignment Guidance Accuracy**
   - The on-screen guidance for centering the face is occasionally inconsistent.
   - In some cases, the system indicates proper alignment when it is slightly off, or vice versa.

3. **Guide Frame Feedback Issues**
   - The alignment frame sometimes remains orange or red even when the face appears correctly positioned.
   - This creates confusion and reduces user trust in the system feedback.

4. **Manual Capture Button Reliability**
   - The capture button appears unresponsive in certain scenarios.
   - Even when alignment looks correct, users may be unable to manually trigger a capture, leading to frustration.

### Mobile Camera & Technical Risks

- **Camera Stability**: Hand movement can affect alignment detection and auto-capture accuracy, especially without stabilization support.
- **Real-Time Detection Limitations**: Variability in lighting, camera quality, and device performance may impact face detection and alignment reliability.
- **False Positives/Negatives**: The system may incorrectly validate or reject alignment due to imperfect detection thresholds.

### Summary
While the overall experience is smooth and user-friendly, improving alignment detection accuracy and providing more reliable capture controls would significantly enhance usability and user confidence.
